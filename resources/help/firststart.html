Indeed, there are no Menus or buttons in Traverso, well, almost no buttons!
However, all the actions are only a fingertip away, read on...

Learning how to use Traverso is as you allready noticed different in respect to other programs.
There isn't the "browse through menus" and "click on buttons" to explore what the program can do for you.
